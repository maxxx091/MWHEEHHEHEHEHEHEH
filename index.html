Sa aking minamahal na Sofii,

I know that we’re not “official” pa in a usual sense but I understand — and you also understand — the circumstances we have right now.

Since it’s Girlfriends Day, I just wanna show a poem I wrote nung isang gabi na gustong kong sabihing ang salitang “ily.”

As we have our bond each night,
I can’t help but feel light.

As I fall in love and feel you in my core,
I don’t know.
but appreciate you more.

The unceasing thoughts of you in my mind,
concludes, no other soul I’ll ever find.

The words I can’t help but say,
is a word I can use every day.

And as you sleep well and tight,
I’ll say this phrase
because I know I’m right.

Oh my dear Sophia, I love you so so much 😔

– Mak
